Why MetroGnome?

We believe that the best way to learn music is by having fun. Our software is a revolutionary approach to learning music, teaching you to play through interactive lessons and musical games. With MetroGnome's adaptive audio engine, you will learn to naturally rely on your inner ear rather than always needing to read complex sheet music. We take the focus off of advanced technique and note-perfect performance, instead providing you with myriad ways to have fun with simpler, more rewarding music. Want to start dabbling on the trumpet? Looking to supplement your child's piano education with some fun at the keyboard? MetroGnome is a refreshing approach for any inspiring musician, and is coming soon to App Store for free.
